Choose Your Sections

Pick 4 sections for Starter or 6 sections for Plus. Each section is a distinct part of your one-page website.

Hero Section

Full-width intro with background image, headline, description, and call-to-action button.

Services

Showcase what you offer with 3-6 service cards, each with icon, title, and description.

About / Company Story

Your story, mission, company history, or what makes you different.

Contact Section

Full contact section with form, map, address, and business hours (beyond basic header).

Testimonials

Customer reviews with photos and names (3 reviews in Starter, 6 in Plus).

Gallery

Photo showcase in a grid layout (5-10 images basic, 20+ extended is addon).

Team / Meet the Owner

Staff or owner profiles with photos and bios (1 person spotlight or full team of 3-6 people).

Pricing / Menu

Display your pricing, packages, or services menu in a clear table format.

Portfolio / Work Examples

Showcase previous projects or work with images and descriptions (3-6 examples).

FAQ

Common questions with accordion-style answers (up to 10 questions).

Process / How It Works

Your workflow or process explained in 3-4 clear steps.

Before/After Showcase

Interactive slider comparing before and after images (ideal for transformations, renovations, treatments).

Stats / Social Proof

Numbers that build trust (e.g., years in business, happy customers, projects completed).

Featured Products

Display 3-6 products with images and descriptions (not ecommerce - display only, no shopping cart).

Certifications / Awards

Display licenses, industry memberships, accreditations, or awards with logos.

Service Area

Show your coverage area with a map or list of suburbs/regions you serve.

Special Offers

Highlight current promotions, seasonal deals, or limited-time offers.

Need more sections? Extra sections beyond your package limit are $100 each. We recommend choosing your package based on how many sections you'll need upfront.
